record rainfall

bridges constricted riverflow
urbanisation caused surface runoff

Social Impacts:
515 properties flooded
Mains water supply unavailable
key access routes flooded
keswick school closed

Economic Impacts:
booths supermarket closed until march
Low briery holiday park lost caravans
A591 severly damaged

Environmental Impacts:
riverbank erosion and landslides
river sediments deposited through keswick

Immediate responses:
Environmental agency closed flood gates and strengthened defences with sandbags
pump set up to pump water back into river
severe flood warning
removed 25k tons of gravel and debris from river greta
1,200 tons of silt removed from keswick
new wider bridges replaced destroyed ones

Long Term Responses:
grants of £500 to homeowners and businesses affected
flood managment in the form of channel maintenance
flood managment in the form of soft engineering strategies including planting 23.5 hectares of trees
